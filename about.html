ABOUT

Rozina Pusztai is a multidisciplinary designer specialising in costume design, textile art, illustration, and creative direction. Her core practice centres on styling for e-commerce, advertising, and social media campaigns, where she combines a distinct visual sensibility with a careful attention to detail.
